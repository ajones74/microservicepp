== Introduction

. Services are ELEMENTS.
. ELEMENTS are data SINKS or data SOURCES.
. ADAPTERS are both data SINKS and data SOURCES.
. PUBLISHERS support multiple data SOURCES.
. SUBSCRIBERS support mulitple data SINKS.
. QUEUES are ADAPTERS with unique support for buffering data.
